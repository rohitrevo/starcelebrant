About Me

The celebrant behind the ceremony

I believe that your wedding ceremony should be one of the most personal experiences of your life — not a script someone else wrote, but a true reflection of who you are, where you've come from, and what you mean to each other.

I'm a Sydney-based, fully registered marriage celebrant with a deep specialisation in multicultural and intimate ceremonies. Over the years I've had the privilege of working with couples from across Australia and around the world, blending traditions ranging from Indian and Lebanese to Chinese, Greek, Filipino, and beyond.

What drives me is simple: helping couples feel completely seen on the most important day of their lives.

What I Believe

Three things that guide every ceremony I create

Deep Listening

Before I write a single word, I spend time truly getting to know you — your story, your families, your humour, your dreams. The ceremony comes from that conversation.

Cultural Respect

Every tradition I incorporate is researched, understood, and handled with genuine respect. I never treat cultural elements as decoration — they're the heart of the ceremony.

Calm Presence

On your wedding day, you shouldn't have to think about the ceremony running smoothly. That's my job. I bring calm, warmth, and quiet confidence so you can simply be present.

Common Questions

Things couples often ask

How far in advance should I book?

Popular dates (especially Saturdays from October–April) can book 12–18 months ahead. I recommend reaching out as soon as you have a date in mind.

Do you travel outside Sydney?

Yes — I regularly travel to the Blue Mountains, Hunter Valley, South Coast, and beyond. Travel fees apply outside the Greater Sydney area.

Can you incorporate our cultural traditions?

Absolutely — it's my speciality. I'll research your traditions thoroughly and work with you to weave them into the ceremony meaningfully.

What legal paperwork is required?

I handle all of it — including the Notice of Intended Marriage (which must be lodged at least one month before the wedding) and the registration of your marriage.

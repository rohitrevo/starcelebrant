Meet Your Celebrant

Ceremonies that feel genuinely yours

I'm a Sydney-based marriage celebrant with a passion for creating ceremonies that truly reflect the people at the centre of them. Having worked with couples from dozens of cultural backgrounds, I understand that no two love stories are ever the same.

My approach is warm, unhurried, and deeply personal. I take the time to get to know you both, so that on your wedding day, every word feels like it was written just for you — because it was.

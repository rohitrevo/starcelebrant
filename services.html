How It Works

From first enquiry to "I do"

  1. Initial EnquiryFill in the contact form or drop me an email. I'll get back to you within 24 hours to arrange a chat.
  2. Getting to Know YouA relaxed consultation where I learn about your story, your vision, and any cultural elements you'd like to incorporate.
  3. Ceremony DesignI write a fully personalised ceremony script and share it with you for feedback. We refine it together until it's perfect.
  4. Legal PaperworkI handle all the required legal notices and documentation — stress-free.
  5. RehearsalWe walk through the ceremony at your venue so everyone knows exactly what to expect.
  6. Your Wedding DayI arrive early, set up calmly, and deliver your ceremony with warmth and professionalism. You just enjoy it.
